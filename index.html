App Features

Never Forget

Digital Dash's main goal is to make sure that your loved ones are never forgotten. Through our application the memories you care most about will be embeded in stone. Our goal is to use the technology that is always surrounding us to make sure your most precious memories are never lost, or forgotten.

Reality Augmented

Our application uses the augmented reality technology to bring your memories as close as possible. By harnessing the power of today's mobile technologies we are able to bring your memories back into the world around you.

Sharing, on Your Terms

Some memories are just to special to share, we get that. That's why Digital Dash has the option to keep your memories private, but still hosted in our application. We want you to have the comfort of knowing those memories are not only stored in our online vault, but they are also kept private. With Digital Dash, you decide who you share your memories with.

Gone but Never Forgotten

A large part of Digital Dash is making sure the history of the Human Race is not forgotten. This includes special attention to those that left us before we were ready. Veterans and young children will always have a special place in each of our hearts, and a special place in Digital Dash.
